Recipient

The homepage for donation recipients is the Dashboard page. You can search for items here and reserve them.

  • View your statistics on the View My Stats page.
  • Manage your reservations on the Reservations page:
    • Mark your orders as picked up.
    • Review picked up donations.
    • Cancel or modify not yet picked up reservations.
  • Edit your profile and set dietary restrictions on the Profile page.
  • Communicate with donors on the Messages page.
    • You should coordinate with the organization through Messages for pickup.

Donor

Manage your donations through the Organizations tab. Donations must be made via an organization, even if the organization is just yourself!

  • View or add organizations on the Organizations page.
  • For each organization:
    • Add donations.
    • View active donations and pending orders.
    • Download all orders (pending, picked up, and canceled).
    • View reviews.
    • Owners can:
      • Edit organization details.
      • Add administrators or other owners.
      • Deactivate the organization.
  • View statistics by clicking View All next to the statistics section.
  • Check messages by clicking Messages on the Manage Organization page.

Community Drive

Manage your community food drives through the Community Drives button.

  • You can start a community drive by clicking
  • You should fill in:
    • Drive name
    • Lead Organization for the Drive
    • The target number of meals and volunteers
    • The dates for the community drive
    • A brief description of the drive
  • Once you submit the community drive, other organizations will be able to pledge meals and volunteers to your community drive.
  • To contribute to the drive, you can click View Drive to view more information and contribute.
  • Under 'My Community Drives', you can also manage or delete Community Drives you have created.
